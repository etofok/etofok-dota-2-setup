How to Last-Hit

Step 1a
EnableAuto-repeat Right Mouse.

Step 1b
EnableQuick Attack.

Step 2
Hover you mouse cursor over the target you want to last-hit.

Step 3
Click and holdRight-Clickon the target.
Attackwon't work for this!
You have to useRight-Click!

Step 4
Spam-tap theStopcommand to cancel the attack animation.

This way you can reliably last-hit most creeps.

ChooseRight-Click Allies: To Attackfor better denying early on.
ChooseRight-Click Allies: To Denyfor better gameplay. Right-Click Allies: To Attackattempts to deny the creep even if it's above 50% health.
The attack animation is repeatedly cancelled until the unit falls below 50% health.

This effectively 'locks' your hero on target at your attack range.

There are disadvantages:

1) You lose the easy "click-to-follow".
2) You have to use theMovebutton to follow an ally or getting close to an ally.
3) Imagine a big fight and you need to run away. If you accidentally click on anything allied instead of the ground — your hero will stop and turn in an attempt to attack whatever you clicked on.

This will surely get you killed, unless you click all things perfectly all the time.
That's why I do not recommend this.

If you chooseRight-Click Allies: To Denyand the target can't be denied — your hero will walk toward it and follow it.

This means you can't 'pre-attack' to immediately start denying, which is a minor disadvantage.

However, there are major advantages:

1) You do not lose the "click-to-follow".
2) You don't need theMovebutton as much.
3) If you accidentally click on an ally instead of ground — your hero will still walk in the general direction you wanted.

ChooseRight-Click Allies: To Attackfor slightly better denying early on.
ChooseRight-Click Allies: To Denyfor better gameplay overall.
