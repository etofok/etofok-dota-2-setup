Chat Wheel

This is not for flavor — this is a mandatory bind. It has to be easily accessible.
Emotional Damage is a real thing, as is Emotional Support.

TheChat Wheelis something that I use all the time, so the hotkey has to be good.

After trying out 10+ different variations — I decided F2 is the best.

  • Chat Wheel 1: F2
  • Chat Wheel 2: F3   or   F2   +   Alt

Chat Wheel 1 Setup

Over a decade I figured this is the required setup to play.

It just makes sense.

GoandGet Backare required for any coordination.
We put them as polar opposites on the wheel (top - bottom).

Group Upserves as the "Get Ready" and "Let's Go" call-outs.
It's also useful when you actually want to group up, i.e. for Smokes

Spread Outis important for any pre-teamfight action, especially when facing big ultimates such as Black Hole, Reverse Polarity, Chronosphere, etc.

Generally useful around objectives, especially high-ground.

Fight Under Towerprobably contributes to half of my MMR.

Fight Under Toweris the ultimate "DO NOT DO ANYTHING STUPID".

- Prepares the team for action.
- Reminds them not to dive.
- Reminds them not to chase off your own high-ground.

It's the best.

All missis a fantastic call-out for "They are smoked", "They are ganking", "Watch the minimap" and "Careful."

I use it all the time when I don't see anyone of the minimap.
I use it even as a morale booster after a team wipe.

It's great!

UseAll misswithFight under tower,Get backandGroup Upto bring your team into safety (results may vary).

I really find these two very valuable:
Nice
Space Created

I useNicewhenever something good (or lucky) happens.
By "good" I mean "in our favor".

This acts as a positive reinforcenment => "good job", "well done", "we're cooking", etc.
It affects morale of the team in a positive way.

I useSpace Createdwhen something bad (or unlucky) happens.
By "bad" I mean "not in our favor".

This phrase is somewhat funny, so it diffuses some of the negativity that builds up.

In order of importance (for me):

1.GoandGet Back
2.Nice
3.All miss
4.Fight Under Tower
5.Group Up
6.Spread Out
7.Space Created

You can alt-click the in-game timer to call-out the current time into chat.
This means you don't have to put this phrase on your chat wheel.

Fun Fact!

Unlike hero, ability or item names — all the chat wheel phrases are localized (translated) to other languages.

And sometimes not exactly accurately.

For example, in RussianSpace createdreads as "Enemies have been distracted", which is sort of "yeah", but "not really".

Chat Wheel 2 Setup

Use whatever, but here's a couple of extra thoughts.

Affirmative— I like this one a lot because it adds to team spirit.
It allows you to visibly agree with a call, giving you a free "chemistry" boost with your teammate.

Now, people are herd animals.
When they see two players agreeing on something, they are much more likely to follow along as well.

This works even better if you're playing with a friend, because you canAffirmativeeach other's calls and rally the team that way.

Avoid using phases like "Well Played!" to be toxic to your team.
Tilt your opponents, not your teammates.
