Microphone

You will play way above your skill level just by communicating well.
And vice versa.

We all met this skilled but toxic player in our games.
The day he learns how much negative impact he has on his team he'll skyrocket in MMR.

You can win harder with good communication.
You can turn games around with good communication.
You can stop games from derailing with good communication.

You can (believe it or not) have fun if you're vibing with randoms.

You can't go wrong if you say...

a) "X is real"
b) "Nice"

Raw information.
Good thing happened.

That's it.

Bonus points if you say the words with a downward inflection →↓

Examples for [a]

- "Sven fresh BKB 9 seconds."↓
- "Tuskar TPed bot."↓
- "Echo slam used, next echo at 18:20."↓

This type of communication cannot be argued with.

This is not a command.
It's not an opinion.
It's not emotionally charged.

Examples for [b]

- "Nice."
- "You're good."
- "Total LEGEND - this Pugna - PAUSE NOW I'm already submitting this clip to Dota 2 massive balls community."

Who cares - have some fun with your video games.

Do you hate it when someone praises you in voice?
Positive encouragement goes a long way.

Even if only for your own sanity.

If you find yourself talking too much - getting emotional, tanking your behavior score, etc - bind your microphone to a key that's hard to use.

Something like Numpad 9, P or F4.

A lot of players would benefit from doing that.
On both ends, really.
