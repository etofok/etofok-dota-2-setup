How to Last-Hit

Step 1a

Enable Auto-repeat Right Mouse.

Step 1b

Enable Quick Attack.

Step 2

Hover you mouse cursor over the target you want to last-hit.

Step 3

Click and hold Right-Click on the target.
Attack won't work for this!

You have to Right-Click

Step 4

Spam-tap the Stop command to cancel the attack animation.

This way you can reliably last-hit most creeps.

Choose Right-Click Allies: To Attack for slightly better denying early on.
Choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny for much better gameplay. Right-Click Allies: To Attack attempts to deny the creep even if it's above 50% health.
The attack animation is repeatedly cancelled until the unit falls below 50% health.

This effectively 'locks' your hero on the target at your attack range.

There are disadvantages:

1) You lose "click-to-follow".
2) You have to use the Move button to follow an ally or getting close to an ally.
3) Imagine a big fight and you need to run away. If you click on an ally instead of the ground, your hero will stop, turn and attempt to attack whatever you clicked on.

This will surely get you in trouble, unless you click all things perfectly all the time.
That's why I do not recommend this.

If you choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny and the target can't be denied you will walk toward it and follow it.

This means you can't 'pre-attack' to immediately start denying, which is a minor disadvantage.

However, there are major advantages:

1) You do not lose "click-to-follow".
2) You don't need the Move button as much.
3) If you accidentally click on an ally instead of the ground, your hero will still walk in the general direction you wanted.

Choose Right-Click Allies: To Attack for slightly better denying early on.
Choose Right-Click Allies: To Deny for much better gameplay overall.
