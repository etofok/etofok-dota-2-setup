Teleport Scroll

  • Teleport Scroll B
  • Teleport to Base B   +   Alt

B for Base, Building and even Boots of Travel.

Alt + B follows naturally as "my Base" and "self-Boots of Travel"

So perfect it hurts.

I have used G for many years (Go Ground), but B seems to be slightly better.

While I don't feel the difference in ergonomics,
B is more visually coherent with the in-game HUD and, well, linguistics.

One thing I noticed is that you don't want to have this key too close to action.
It's a very expensive misclick.

And just a heads up,
make sure you use Normal Cast, not Quick Cast here.
