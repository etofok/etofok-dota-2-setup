Chat Wheel Extras

But here's a couple of extra thoughts.

I like Affirmative because it adds to team spirit.
It allows you to visibly agree with a call, giving you a free chemistry boost with your teammate.

People are herd animals.
When they see two players agreeing on something, they are much more likely to follow along as well.

So Affirmative works amazingly well when you play with a friend. You can just Affirmative each other calls and rally your team that way.

Avoid using phases like "Well Played!" to be toxic to your team.
Tilt your opponents, not your teammates.

As you can see, my Chat Wheel 2 is quite empty.
