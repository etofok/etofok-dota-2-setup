Mouse wheel

While we're at it, In the age of scrolling it makes sense to increase your scroll speed from "3" to at least "6".

Mouse wheel "buttons" can also be bound:

Mouse Wheel Up
Mouse Wheel Down
Alt + Mouse Wheel Up
Alt + Mouse Wheel Down

And even Mouse Wheel Click
Alt + Mouse Wheel Click
Although this one I would avoid as much as possible in favor of Edge Pan

These are actually pretty good binds.

However, these are very "personal" binds that are impossible to "transfer" from one individual to another without raising eyebrows.

But I'm sure some of you dear readers can make great use of it.

For example,
these binds are absolute life-savers for people who can't use their second hand - in case of a disability or injury.

Which means they have to stick with the mouse only.
