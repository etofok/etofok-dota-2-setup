Abilities Require Cancel

You want this Enabled to prevent wasting your important channeled spells such as Black Hole, Freezing Field or Fiend's Grip

A word of caution for Puck players.
Phase Shift is a channel!

If you have Abilities Require Cancel Enabled then you have to manually Stop Phase Shift every time before Ethereal Jaunt.

This is unreasonably clunky and you will die a lot because of it.
