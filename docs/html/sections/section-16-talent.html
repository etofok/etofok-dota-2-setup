Talents

Upgrade Talentis not something that you use often.
Literally up to 8 times in a very long game.

But there was a period of my life when I was a Turbo enjoyer, where it's not rare to get a big level up in the middle of a fight.

That's how I found out that having a hotkey forUpgrade Talentis more comfortable than not having it. I felt annoyed I had to press the "plus sign" with the cursor. This sounds ridiculous as I write this but it's true.

Upgrade Talentis great on ` (grave), because you then select the left-or-right talent with 1 or 2 which are right next to it.
It's instant and makes perfect sense.

  • Upgrade Talents: `
  • Upgrade Left Talent: 1
  • Upgrade Right Talent: 2
  • Upgrade Attributes: 3   or   none

Please note!

The hotkey will always get the talent in order of their appearance in the 'tree'.

If you have multiple talents available i.e. both 10 and 15 talents â€” the hotkey will select the level 10 talent first.

You may also want to unbindUpgrade Attributesto prevent accidentally selecting it.
