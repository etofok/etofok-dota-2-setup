Preface

Don't worry, there will be a plenty of images.
But let me say something before we begin.

Your hotkeys should make sense:
- simple to understand
- easy to remember
- fast to press

The more intuitive they are, the fewer mistakes you'll make.
And you'll execute your decisions faster.

Even if your decisions aren't great - at least you won't be limited by doing them.

Your hotkey layout is a direct bridge between your brain and the game.

It's the only way to interact with it.

But when you plug a device into a computer you never want it to run slower, do you?
Why be slow when we can be quick?

On this page, you'll find a very detailed explanation of what I consider the best Dota 2 hotkeys, settings and options possible.

You will understand the logic behind each and every decision for every hotkey and most in-game settings.
And you will learn a few very niche tricks that 99.99% don't know about.

So,

If you feel like your setup doesn't really make sense,
Or if you're looking for a potentially lifelong improvement in your play

You will get all this (and more) just by reading this page and this is my promise.
