Auto attack

UseAuto Attack: Neverfor Tinkerand Earth Spirit

UseAuto Attack: Standardfor everything else.

You definitely want to haveSummoned Unit Auto Attack: Alwaysfor manta and illusion based heroes such as Phantom Lancer, Terrorbladeand Naga Siren.

This works:Auto Attack: Standard+Summoned Unit Auto Attack: Always

Another option is to useToggle Autoattackbutton.

This allow you to useSummoned Unit Auto Attack: Same as Heroand switch the state with the button.

However, I personally don't likeAutoattack: Onon my main hero.
I useAuto Attack: Standard+Summoned Unit Auto Attack: Always.

Experiment with it.

And don't forget to enableQuick AttackandQuick Move.
