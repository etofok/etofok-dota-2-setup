Abilities 5 - 6

QWER are very good keys.

T is a very good key.

But what about Ability 6?

Besides,
not every hero even has Ability 5 or Ability 6.

And when they do - some abilities are very important and some are barely used.

Some abilities are passives.

Is it even possible to make a layout considering all these variables?
I believe I found a solution.

I'm going to start with a simple example.

Сoup De Grace is a passive ability.
You can't activate it.
It's a passive.

Сoup De Grace on R is a waste of a good key.

So, let's move Fan of Knives to R instead.
We just made it easier for ourselves to use Fan of Knives

Another example, Ogre Magi

Multicast is a passive ability.
You can't activate it.

So let's move Fire Shield to R in its place, why not?

This is reasonable, because both of our 'buff abilities' Bloodlust and Fire Shield are next to each other and use very good keys ER.

Important!

You still want a hotkey for your passive abilities!
If you don't have a hotkey, you can't CTRL + Ability Key to upgrade the ability.

You don't need a great key, but you do need a key.

Here I want to highlight something...

Despite that we have Control Group 3 bound to 3, we can use 3 as an ability key!

It is possible to bind abilities to control group keys!

The ability has higher priority.

That's fantastic news, because most heroes have no use for this many control groups, so keys like 345 are essentially free real estate.

Now equipped with this new knowledge let's revisit our Ogre Magi

There's a small problem.

Specifically 4Fire Shield.

You don't know it yet, but we will use ALT12345 as our Shop keys.
Sorry for the spoiler!

That's why we don't want to use 12345 for abilities that can be ALT casted or toggled.

Fire Shield is one of these abilities.

Which means you don't want to have it on 12345.
Which means it has to be either R or T
Which means it is objectively the best on R.

This minor nitpick plays also plays a role for Invoker.

As pointed out by Streamors, the extra problem with Alacrity is that it can be in either ability slot due to the nature of the hero, which means that we can't use 34 for invoked spells in our configuration.
