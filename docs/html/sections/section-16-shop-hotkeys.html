Shop Hotkeys

Here's the simplest way to go about all-things-shop:

  • Select Courier: `

  • Open Shop Alt   +   1
  • Quickbuy Alt   +   2
  • Courier Deliver Alt   +   3
  • Courier Burst Alt   +   4
  • Buy Sticky Alt   +   5

In any case you want the following pattern:
Quickbuy → Courier Deliver → Courier Burst

And it doesn't get any simpler than
ALT2 → 3 → 4


Putting Open Shop in front of this pattern makes the most sense as our "main" shop-related action.

So we end up with
ALT1 → 2 → 3 → 4

Select Courier` sits on our Control Group row 1234
After all - we are selecting a unit.

Select Courier` also sits on our all-things-shop row Alt1234
After all - we are doing something related to the shop.

So this is literally perfect.

Buy Sticky

Buy Sticky used to be much more useful before we weren't getting free Teleport Scrolls on deaths.
This was introduced in the 7.29 update (along with Dawnbreaker and Water Runes)

It's still useful though.
Especially for core players, because they - usually and hopefully - die less than they teleport around.

Long story short, bind Buy Sticky to ALT5 as a "low priority extra shop related action".

  • Open Shop Alt   +   1
  • Quickbuy Alt   +   2
  • Courier Deliver Alt   +   3
  • Courier Burst Alt   +   4
  • Buy Sticky Alt   +   5

I haven't mentioned it yet but you might want to bind Take Stash Items.

Almost all item transfers happen through Alt3 Courier Deliver, even at the fountain.

So this action is only needed when you are at the fountain area, but not the courier.

Which is realistically at the enemy fountain or if the courier is dead.

I just use Alt6.
